"Meet a shining young star with a heart full of charm."

Precious Annisa Dalimunthe, affectionately known as Annisa Dalimunthe in Indonesia and Precious Annisa in Malaysia, is a bright and talented child who continues to capture hearts across social media.

Born on 5 February 2019, Annisa represents a new generation of young content creators with natural charisma and authentic appeal. Based between Kuala Lumpur and Jakarta, she bridges two vibrant cultures.

Active on Instagram since June 2024, her journey has grown rapidly and organically, achieving 4,248 organic followers while reaching an impressive 4 million accounts — an incredible milestone for a young talent.
